What is this website?

This is my website, hosted on GitHub, that is used as a pseudo-portfolio for some of my past works, as well as demonstrates a rough outline of who I am, what I am interested in, and certain skills that I have. More will be added to this site as time progresses and I have more free time to upload new files (and design new pages). In the meantime, there is the About Me section, which briefly details my education, experience, and what positions I am interest in for employment. On this page there is also some information in order to contact me, such as my email, as well as my LinkedIn and GitHub pages.

There is also a Past Projects section that includes a handful of previous assignments and projects, as well as some files, presentations, and code from said projects. Most of these projects were coded in different languages, from C++ to Python to Swift, in order to highlight the various languages I have experience with. More projects will be added, including demos of Unity projects and code in other languages, but meanwhile the About Me page offers a more in-depth explanation regarding the years of experience that I have with each language.
